A perfume is a very complex composition of top notes, heart notes and base notes. The top notes are the most delicate and do typically not last very long.

Heart notes tend to be herbal, floral and spice notes which last longer than the top notes but you will be able to smell the base notes the longest.

The word perfume comes from the last it makes us feel good all day Today, perfumes are complex and made up of many natural and synthetic chemicals,
